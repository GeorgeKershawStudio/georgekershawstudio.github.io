Edinburgh Council Banner Project

A banner project completed for the Edinburgh Council's Granton Restoration Project

The Competition

The Edinburgh Council gave a short competition brief to the FA Graphic Design students at the Edinburgh College. I ultimately won the competition and I worked alongside my tutor and the council in a live project to refine my initial designs.

I proposed several styles and configurations of banners, along with ways of them being displayed

Once the designs were finalised, the banners were displayed outside the Granton Gas Tower, and I worked with the publicity team to showcase them.
