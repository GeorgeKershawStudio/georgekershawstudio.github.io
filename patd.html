PatD Concert Collection

Extra-Curricular Project at the Edinburgh College

While studying at Edinburgh College, I completed my assigned projects early and moved on to an extra project for the remainder of the year. An earlier project was to design a promotional poster for a band having a concert at the Edinburgh Castle, and so my extra-curricular project was to expand on that initial poster into a whole suite.

The initial poster featured the band's name depicted as a towering castle, with their signiture colourful chaos surrounding it. To expand on this concept, I designed a ticket to go alongside it, followed by a CD case and disk design that used imagery from one of their songs.

Magazine

The major piece of this project was a 12 page magazine section for the concert, fully designed and written by myself.

This would act as an advertisement section for a regualr magazine, including information on the band, concert, and castle.
